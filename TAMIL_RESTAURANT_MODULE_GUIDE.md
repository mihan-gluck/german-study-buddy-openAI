## ğŸŒ Cultural Notes

- **Politeness**: English-speaking countries emphasize "please" and "thank you"
- **Tipping**: Common practice (10-15% in most countries)
- **Recommendations**: It's normal to ask waiters for suggestions
- **Seating**: Wait to be seated in formal restaurants
- **Bill Splitting**: Common among friends (unlike traditional Tamil culture)

## ğŸ’¡ Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Forgetting politeness words**: Always use "please" and "thank you"
2. **Direct Tamil translation**: Avoid word-for-word translation
3. **Using "I want"**: Prefer "I would like" for politeness
4. **Modal verb confusion**: Learn when to use "can" vs "may"
